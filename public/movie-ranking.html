The Best Movies Of All Time According To Giann

My top 4 movies of all time.

Back To The Future I (1985)

Incredible Sci-Fi movie about time travel filled with drama, action, and science. It Revolves around a kid who accidentally changes
the course of history by separating his parents' meeting and must do everything in his power to reunite them or risk never existing.

Drumline (2002)

Incredibly fun and heartwarming film involving a young musician trying to make a name for himself by being the best
while struggling with his past and a secret that could destroy his dreams.

The Blind Side (2009)

Very emotional movie revolving around a family that takes in a troubled child with a big heart and kind soul
who ends up achieving greatness.

Dickie Roberts: Former Child Stars (2003)

Very funny film starring David Spade about a Child Star who is trying to make a comeback in all the wrong ways and
has a chance to star in a make-or-break film but needs to be attuned to how to be a real child, so he makes a plan
to hire a family to help him relive a 'normal' childhood.
